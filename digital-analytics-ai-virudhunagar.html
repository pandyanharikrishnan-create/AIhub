Digital Analytics with AI Training in Virudhunagar

Master web analytics, conversion rate optimization, and AI-driven user behavior tracking to make data-informed marketing decisions.

Foundations

The Power of Data-Driven Digital Analytics

In modern business, decisions shouldn't be based on guesses. Digital analytics tracking tells you exactly who your users are, where they come from, and what causes them to buy. With AI, analytics goes further, predicting future behaviors and highlighting friction points instantly.

Our Digital Analytics with AI program focuses on setting up and managing robust analytics pipelines.

You will learn to configure Google Analytics 4 (GA4), set up tracking events via Google Tag Manager, design conversion rate optimization (CRO) tests, and build automated Looker Studio dashboards.

  • Master GA4 setup, custom events, and user conversion funnels
  • Learn GTM tag configurations and triggers for tracking clicks
  • Conduct A/B testing and design conversion optimization experiments
  • Build interactive Looker Studio dashboards with AI insight widgets
  • Explore predictive metrics and behavior modeling features in GA4
  • Analyze actual site traffic logs and build detailed reports

Specialization

Understand Every Click

We focus heavily on hands-on technical tracking. You won't just learn to read reports; you will build the tracking pipelines yourself. You will map user events, write custom triggers, run experiments, and automate executive dashboards.

This combination of tracking knowledge and AI analytics ensures you stand out in the technical marketing space.

  • Map custom e-commerce purchase events
  • Set up page scroll depth tracking triggers
  • Build conversion funnel visualization flowcharts
  • Configure user consent states for tracking cookies
  • E-commerce GA4 Pipeline Setup: Install GA4 and set up purchase event tracking for a mockup shop.
  • Looker Studio Executive Dashboard: Build a single-page automated dashboard reporting traffic, CPC, and ROI.
  • CRO A/B Testing Report: Design, run, and document an A/B test comparing two call-to-action buttons.
  • User Behavior Audit Report: Use session logs and heatmaps to find and explain usability friction points.
